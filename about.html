I discovered why I like miniature life by Tanaka Tatsuya,

IBM's People For Smarter Cities Initiative designed by Ogilvy,

Marcel Duchamp's Fountain,

and Rirkrit Tiravanija's Untitled (Free) exhibition converting a gallery into kitchen serving free rice and Thai curry.

Tatsuya plays with scale, making analogies between daily-use objects and larger experiences. Ogilvy breathes ingenuity into IBM's campaign my making ads functional. Duchamp pokes fun at who determines what is art by declaring a urinal a piece of art. Tiravanija wonders if anything inside a museum can be deemed art. All of these disparate works are connected by creativity, the ability to tie disparate things together and see things in a new light.
