The Summer of 2019, I interned at DCTV and had the opportunity to shoot my own documentary. As every Asian American experience is different, I decided to document the lives of family friends and friends. My goal was to deep dive into childhood experiences of those my parents' generation and of those of my generation to truly learn who these people are and what experiences shaped their being. The resulting interviews left me astonished, as if I was meeting these people all over again.
