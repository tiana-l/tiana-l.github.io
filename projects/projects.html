  • pesKey bot

Always forget your keys? Meet pesKey bot, a reminder bot with a bit of sass. Now, your partner never has to wave their arms in defeat or scream at you to take your keys. pesKey bot does that for them.

How pesKey bot came into being

pesKey bot was inspired by Beauty and the Beast's Lumiere. We wanted to make an object come to life, give an object its own personality. We hoped to create an object users would have a deeper level of interaction with.

Interaction is the receiving, processing, and communication of information between two or more parties /objects. pesKey bot increases human object interaction by giving pesKey bot, an object, with more ways to sense and communicate with humans. pesKey bot is able to process your distance from it and whether you took your keys or not, and communicate different messages accordingly.

For further implementation, we hope to create a design that makes pesKey's key holder function more obvious and be able to add more sassy messages that pesKey bot can use to remind you to take your keys.
